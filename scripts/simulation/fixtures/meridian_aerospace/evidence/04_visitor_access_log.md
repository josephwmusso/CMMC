content_summary: |
  The Visitor Access Log is a single-sheet Excel spreadsheet covering
  January through March 2026. It is a digitized version of the paper
  sign-in binder kept at the front desk of Meridian Aerospace's combined
  office and shop floor facility.

  The spreadsheet contains 47 visitor entries across the quarter. Each row
  records: visitor name, company or affiliation, date of visit, time in,
  time out, person visited (Meridian employee host), and a free-text
  "purpose" column. Common visitor purposes include vendor deliveries,
  equipment service calls, customer part pickups, and a handful of
  job-candidate interviews.

  The data shows that most visits are during normal business hours
  (7:00 AM – 4:30 PM). Several entries have blank "time out" fields,
  indicating the sign-out step is inconsistently enforced. Approximately
  8 of the 47 entries are missing the time-out value. Host employee names
  are present for all entries.

  The spreadsheet was created by manually transcribing the paper binder
  entries at the end of each month. There is no automated visitor
  management system in use.

  DELIBERATELY ABSENT: There is no electronic badge or access-card system.
  Visitors are not issued temporary badges and there is no photographic
  identification verification noted. There is no column for ID type
  checked or badge number issued. There is no record of escort
  requirements being enforced — the log does not indicate whether visitors
  were escorted or had unescorted access to the shop floor. There is no
  restricted-area designation or CUI-area access control. There is no
  integration with any electronic access control or surveillance system.
  Prior quarters' logs (before January 2026) have not been digitized and
  exist only as paper binders in a filing cabinet.
