Coastal Shield's IR plan is a 15-page document covering detection,
containment, eradication, recovery, and lessons learned phases. The
plan references DFARS 252.204-7012 72-hour notification requirements.
Contact list includes BlueVoyant MSP emergency line and DIBCAC.
NOTE: The plan has NOT been tested via tabletop exercise since initial
publication in June 2025. No evidence of any IR drill or simulated
incident exercise exists. This is a known gap — the plan exists on
paper but operational readiness is unverified.
